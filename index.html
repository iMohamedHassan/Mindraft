About us

Mind Draft is an independent website developed by Mohamed Hassan and designed by Kamal.
Our goal is to provide useful content and tools that help users and designers turn their ideas into clear and simple outcomes.
The project was started without external funding, but with a focus on providing a clean, organized, and helpful user experience.
We’re continuously working on improving and updating the platform based on user needs.
